Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

1. Introduction

At Digital Earnings, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Advertisements & Monetization (Important)

We believe in transparency. Our mission is to provide premium educational content (eBooks and Guides) absolutely free of charge. To make this possible, we are supported by third-party advertisers.

We use "Content Locking" technology. This means that to access certain premium downloads, you may be asked to complete a short promotional offer (such as a survey, app download, or email submission) from our advertising partners.

This is a value exchange: Instead of paying with money, you "pay" with a moment of your time. This revenue allows us to maintain our servers and continue creating high-quality content for you.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect information in the following ways:

  • Voluntary Information: Name or email address if you contact us directly.
  • Third-Party Data: When you complete an offer to unlock content, the advertising network may collect data required to verify the completion of the task. We do not store this personal data on our servers.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to analyze traffic and improve user experience.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party websites (Advertisers/Offers). We have no control over the content and privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you interact with.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our monetization methods, please contact us via our Contact Page.
